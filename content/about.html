Heidi Erdmann

Heidi Erdmann has been active in the South African art-world since 1993. Her experience prior to opening her own gallery includes three years at the South African National Gallery, Cape Town, from 1994 to 1997 and Curator at AREA Gallery from 1997 - 1999. Over the past twenty two years, she curated more than 200 exhibitions, working both locally and abroad, and has helped launch the careers of many of South Africa's top artists.

She is the Director of Erdmann Contemporary.
