Melanie Cleary

View Gallery

Born in 1970. Lives and works in Cape Town, South Africa.

Cleary is a self-taught photographer who started her career in 2001. She honed her skills on film sets while shooting stills and developed an ability to capture a narrative portrait. Her career as a fine art photographer took off in 2009 with her debut solo exhibition, The Hero Within, which received critical acclaim and was exhibited at international festivals, art fairs and galleries.

Cleary’s interest lies with the Other: those on the fringes of society; those who would usually be over-looked or ignored - the marginalized. Similarly, she finds her projects and inspiration in unexpected moments and places, unsolicited and unplanned. Her latest series, Another Day, shares a similar genesis: the project found her. This time, inspiration presented itself in the form of undated, black and white contact sheet prints framed and installed in the foyer of one of the oldest special needs children’s homes in South Africa. Another Day illustrates her strength as remarkable portraitist.
