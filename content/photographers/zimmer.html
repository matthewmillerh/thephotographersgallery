Niklas Zimmer

View Gallery

Born in 1975 in Bangkok, Thailand. Lives and works in Cape Town, South Africa.

German-South African artist Niklas Zimmer mainly works in photography, sound and performance. He aims to communicate a certain joie de vivre that takes cognisance of all the contradictions and failures experienced both in life and in art.

In order to achieve this, Zimmer takes particular interest in moments were all unplanned elements come to play their essential part in a very controlled set-up. In such cases, art isnâ€™t mimicking life, but declares itself part of life. It is in these moments that he seeks his inspiration.
