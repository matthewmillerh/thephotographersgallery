Nic Bothma

View Gallery

Born in 1969. Lives and works in Cape Town, South Africa.

Nic Bothma has worked in more than 60 countries over the past two decades and has won numerous international awards for his work. He began his career in photojournalism by documenting the fall of apartheid and covering the transition into democracy for both local newspapers and international agencies.

His interest lies with documenting the extreme, and sharing stories of the enduring human spirit. His own journey as a long distance solo sailor has taken him across both the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, while documenting remote island cultures.

He is presently the Chief Photographer, West Africa for European Press Photo Agency (EPA).
