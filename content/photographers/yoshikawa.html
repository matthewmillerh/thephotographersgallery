Naoya Yoshikawa

View Gallery

Born in 1961 in Nara, Japan. Lives and works in Toyonaka, Osaka.

Professor of Photography at Takarazuka University and Dean of the School of Arts and Design in his home country, Naoya Yoshikawa has held more than 20 solo exhibitions from as early as 1983 and has participated in numerous international group exhibitions since 1985. His latest series, Family Album, comments on the ever-evolving history of photography as a medium of expression through the action of capturing light - a theme that Yoshikawa has actively been exploring since 2001.

Yoshikawa also served as the Director of MIO Photo Award and MIO Photo Osaka from 1998 to 2012.
