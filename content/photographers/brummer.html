Paris Brummer

View Gallery

Born in 1992. Lives and works in Cape Town, South Africa.

Paris Brummer is a final year fine arts student at Michaelis School of Fine Art, majoring in photography. Work of hers has been selected for the SASOL New Signatures Exhibition two years in a row.

The cinematic style of her content-rich imagery is the result of her part-time employment in a video store and her life-long love of the cinema. She seeks her inspiration in the enigma and mystery of the still image and the invisible frame that surrounds it, in opposition to the overflow of information that often accompanies films and moving images. The result is a constant absorption of scenes, angles and film stills that provides just the right amount of information to encourage the viewer to complete the narrative independently.
