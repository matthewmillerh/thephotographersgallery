Gary van Wyk

View Gallery

Born in 1981. Lives and works in Cape Town, South Africa.

After working as a news photographer for half a decade and many travels abroad, Van Wyk’s interest lies with street photography, portraiture and the decisive moment. His photographs explore the relationship between private moments and public spaces, and how these two concepts can and do intersect. Van Wyk captures the unseen, the unnoticed and unexpected detail in our built environment, as well as extraordinary moments of human interactions. His photographs are like short stories - each frame is densely layered, filled with narrative and emotion.

Recently, Van Wyk has ventured into self-portraiture, a genre that affords him an opportunity to explore the creative aspects of photography. His resulting Self-Portrait series is just that - a complex exploration of the medium’s aesthetic capacity and its boundaries.
