Pierre Crocquet Estate releases work

The Photographers Gallery ZA, in association with the Crocquet Estate, is pleased to announce the release of a selection of limited edition prints drawn from several series of work by South African photographer Pierre Crocquet. The printing was supervised by Andreas Kahlau from Silvertone Fine Art Printing in Johannesburg, who had worked with and printed most of Crocquet's photographs until his untimely death in 2013. The prints are sold with a certificate of authenticity, issued by the Crocquet Estate and is available from the Photographers Gallery ZA in Cape Town. Pierre Crocquet was part of the The Photographers Gallery ZA launch exhibition when it opened in 2001.

Erdmann joins Critical Mass jurors

Heidi Erdmann has recently been invited to serve on the panel of jurors for Critical Mass, an annual online program, founded in the United States, that builds connections within the global photography community. Photographers at any level, from anywhere in the world, submit a portfolio of 10 images. Through a pre-screening process, the field is narrowed to a group of 200 finalists who go on to have their work viewed and voted on by over 200 esteemed international photography professionals, of which Erdmann is now a part.
